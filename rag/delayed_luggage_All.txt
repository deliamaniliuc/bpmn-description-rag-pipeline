Description:
When the passenger notices their baggage is missing at the arrival belt, they must report it immediately. The passenger goes to the baggage tracing desk in the arrival area. There, the passenger reports the delayed baggage. The staff records the details and provides the passenger with a reference number. Afterward, the airline starts searching for the baggage using the worldwide baggage tracing system. Once the baggage is found, the airline informs the passenger. Finally, the baggage is delivered to the address provided by the passenger.

Description:
When the passenger arrives at the boarding gate, the ground staff checks the flight occupancy. If the flight is not highly occupied, the passenger proceeds to board the aircraft with their carry-on baggage. If the flight is highly occupied, there is too much carry-on baggage for the cabin. In this case, the passenger must check in their compliant carry-on bag. First, the passenger removes hazardous items (like power banks and e-cigarettes) and valuables from the bag. Then, the passenger hands the bag to the gate agent. The bag is tagged and loaded into the cargo hold. Finally, the passenger boards the aircraft without the carry-on bag.
